起卦后得到本卦变卦和选定的卦辞或爻辞，结合我们当下的生存处境，从整体格局上做深度剖析思考。

占卜的真正价值不在于卦爻辞字面意思，而是与先古圣贤神通。文王囚羑里而演周易，为周朝八百年的江山奠定了坚实的基础。周易是一整套曾经真实撑起中国历史上存续最久的王朝的判断经验，不是空穴来风的迷信。

把这样的判断框架叠加在重要决定之上，并不能保证结果一定"吉"，正如之前统计过的：貞吉与貞凶共享同一个"貞"字，具体走向仍取决于时位。占问真正提高的，不是命运的确定性，而是决策过程本身的严谨程度——就像"无咎"是周易里出现最多的判断，它关心的从来是行为有没有破绽。多一层深思，多一次核对，胜算自然会跟着提高。

解卦的本质就是在我们重大决策之前，作深入的风险评估情境分析，以期达到无咎趋吉避凶厥孚大有的结果。

After casting the hexagram, you get an original hexagram, a changed hexagram, and a selected judgment or line text. Combine these with your present real-world situation, and dig deep into the overall pattern before drawing any conclusion.

The real value of divination isn't in the literal wording of the judgment — it's in reaching across time to commune with the ancient sages. King Wen, imprisoned at Youli, worked out the Zhouyi there, laying a solid foundation for eight hundred years of Zhou rule. The Zhouyi is a whole body of judgment and experience that once genuinely upheld the longest-reigning dynasty in Chinese history — not baseless superstition.

Laying this judgment framework over an important decision does not guarantee the outcome will be favorable ("吉"/auspicious) — as the earlier word-frequency count showed, 貞吉 and 貞凶 share the same character 貞, and which way it goes still depends on the specific position and timing. What divination genuinely improves isn't certainty about fate, but the rigor of the decision-making process itself — much like 无咎 ("no fault"), the single most frequent judgment in the Zhouyi, which has always been concerned with whether one's conduct holds up, not with some predetermined outcome. One more layer of deep thought, one more round of cross-checking, and the odds naturally improve.

At its core, interpreting a hexagram means doing deep risk assessment and situational analysis before a major decision — in pursuit of an outcome that is 无咎 (without fault), that moves toward good fortune and away from ill, and that reflects 厥孚大有 (utmost sincerity, great abundance).
